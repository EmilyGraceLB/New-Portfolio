The Technical Challenges & Solutions

Hurdle 1: VR Frame Rate Optimization

The Problem: Heavy asset geometry from high-fidelity 3D meshes caused sudden frame drops under SteamVR rendering passes, inducing mild motion sickness.

The Solution: Re-imported all environment assets back into Blender to aggressively decimate geometry loops, bake high-poly lighting data into static lightmaps, and utilize aggressive occlusion culling within Unity.

Hurdle 2: Physics-Based Spatial Interaction

The Problem: Standard Raycast interaction models felt detached and broke immersion during spatial menu selection routines.

The Solution: Implemented a completely proximity-based physical collision system utilizing Unity's Input System events and physical trigger volumes to make buttons physically depress when touched.
